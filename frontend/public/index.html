Murray State University Department of Computer Science and Information Systems Research Participation Consent Form Study Title: A Study in Service Representatives Primary Investigators: Rizwan Syed (Student Investigator) and Hassan Mistareehi, Computer Science and Information Systems Faculty Sponsor: Hassan Mistareehi, (859) 608-0656, hmistareehi@murraystate.edu You are being invited to participate in a research study conducted through Murray State University. This form contains information you will need to help you decide whether to be in this research study or not. You must be at least 18 years old to participate. Please read the form carefully and ask the study team member(s) questions about anything that is not clear. You will be given a copy of this form to keep. 1. Nature and Purpose of Project: The purpose of this study is to understand which type of customer service portal is preferred by customers. 2. Explanation of Procedures: The study activities include following a script provided by the investigator in a service portal interaction with a customer service representative and the completion of a short survey. Study duration: Your participation in the study is not expected to exceed 15 minutes. 3. Discomforts and Risks: The possible risks and/or discomforts associated with the being in the study include: frustration or discomfort relating to the responses from the service representatives. All responses from online participants will be treated confidentially and stored on an encrypted hard drive. However, we are unable to guarantee the security of the computer on which you choose to enter your responses. Information (or data) you enter, and websites you visit online can be tracked, captured, corrupted, lost, or otherwise misused. The identifying data in the study will be kept on campus for at least three years before being destroyed, as per Murray State University’s policy. 4. Benefits: This study is not designed to benefit you directly. However, your participation may help to increase our understanding of high-quality customer service portals. 5. Confidentiality: Your identity will be known to the researchers, but the information you provide will be kept confidential and will not be traceable back to you. 6. Refusal/Withdrawal: Your participation is strictly voluntary and you are free to withdraw/stop participating at any time with absolutely no penalty. Feel free to skip any questions that you do not wish to answer or to get up and leave at any time. 7. Contact Information: Any questions about the procedures or conduct of this research should be brought to the attention of Dr. Hassan Mistareehi at (859) 608-0656 or hmistareehi@murraystate.edu. If you would like to know the results of this study, please contact Dr. Hassan Mistareehi. Clicking the button below indicates that this study has been explained to you, that your questions have been answered, and that you agree to take part in this study. This project has been reviewed and approved by the Murray State University Institutional Review Board (IRB) for the Protection of Human Subjects. If you have any questions about your rights as a research participant, you should contact the MSU IRB Coordinator at (270) 809-2916 or msu.irb@murraystate.edu.
